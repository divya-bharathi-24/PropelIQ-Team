# User Story - US_013

## Story ID

- ID Format: US_013

## Story Title

- Insurance Pre-Check

## Description

- As a patient, I want my insurance eligibility verified before or during the booking process, so that I know my coverage status and can avoid unexpected out-of-pocket costs.

## Acceptance Criteria

### AC-1: Insurance details capture during booking

- **Given**: A patient is in the appointment booking flow
- **When**: They reach the insurance verification step
- **Then**: A form captures insurance provider, policy number, group number, and member ID with format validation per common insurance ID patterns

### AC-2: Eligibility check returns coverage status

- **Given**: A patient has entered valid insurance details
- **When**: The eligibility check is triggered
- **Then**: Within 5 seconds, the system returns one of: "Active - Covered", "Active - Partial Coverage", "Inactive", or "Unable to Verify", displaying copay estimate if available

### AC-3: Coverage information displayed before booking confirmation

- **Given**: The eligibility check returns a result
- **When**: The patient reviews the booking summary
- **Then**: Coverage status, estimated copay, and any coverage limitations are displayed prominently before the final "Confirm Booking" button

### AC-4: Insurance details saved to patient profile

- **Given**: A patient completes insurance verification successfully
- **When**: The booking is confirmed
- **Then**: The verified insurance details are saved to their profile for future bookings, with a "last verified" timestamp, eliminating re-entry for subsequent appointments

## Edge Cases

- Insurance verification service timeout → proceed with booking, mark insurance as "verification pending", and retry asynchronously within 1 hour
- Patient has multiple insurance plans → allow primary and secondary insurance entry; check primary first, secondary if primary denies
- Insurance expired between verification and appointment date → re-verify 24 hours before appointment; notify patient if status changed
