# User Story - US_012

## Story ID

- ID Format: US_012

## Story Title

- Appointment Management & PDF Confirmation

## Description

- As a patient, I want to view, reschedule, and cancel my appointments with PDF confirmation generation, so that I have full control over my bookings and a downloadable record the confirmation.

## Acceptance Criteria

### AC-1: Patient appointment list view

- **Given**: An authenticated patient has booked appointments
- **When**: They navigate to "My Appointments"
- **Then**: All appointments are displayed in a list with status (confirmed, completed, cancelled, no-show), date/time, provider, and type, sortable by date with pagination (10 per page)

### AC-2: Appointment rescheduling

- **Given**: A patient has a confirmed appointment more than 24 hours in the future
- **When**: They click "Reschedule" and select a new available slot
- **Then**: The original slot is released, the new slot is reserved atomically, status remains "confirmed" with a reschedule count incremented, and a confirmation email is sent

### AC-3: Appointment cancellation with policy enforcement

- **Given**: A patient wants to cancel an appointment
- **When**: They click "Cancel" and confirm
- **Then**: If more than 24 hours before the appointment, cancellation succeeds immediately; if within 24 hours, a warning is displayed about potential no-show impact; the slot is released back to the availability pool

### AC-4: PDF confirmation generation

- **Given**: A patient has a confirmed appointment
- **When**: They click "Download Confirmation"
- **Then**: A PDF is generated containing appointment details (provider, date, time, location, preparation instructions), patient name, and a QR code for check-in, downloadable within 3 seconds

### AC-5: Confirmation email with ICS attachment

- **Given**: An appointment is booked or rescheduled
- **When**: The booking is confirmed
- **Then**: An email is sent via SendGrid within 60 seconds containing appointment details and an ICS calendar file attachment for one-click calendar addition

## Edge Cases

- Reschedule attempt for appointment less than 24 hours away → allow with warning "Late reschedule may affect your reliability score"
- PDF generation service temporarily unavailable → queue for retry; show "PDF will be available shortly" with email delivery fallback
- Patient cancels all appointments rapidly (potential abuse) → allow up to 3 cancellations per day; 4th triggers soft-block requiring staff intervention
