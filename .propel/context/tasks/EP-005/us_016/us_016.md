# User Story - US_016

## Story ID

- ID Format: US_016

## Story Title

- AI Conversational Intake

## Description

- As a patient, I want to complete my pre-appointment intake through an AI-powered conversational interface that asks contextual follow-up questions based on my responses, so that the intake process is faster, more engaging, and captures clinically relevant information without redundant questions.

## Acceptance Criteria

### AC-1: Conversational intake initiated before appointment

- **Given**: A patient has a confirmed appointment within 48 hours
- **When**: They open the intake section or click the intake reminder link
- **Then**: A chat-style interface presents the first intake question personalized to the appointment type (e.g., "What brings you in today?" for general, "Describe your symptoms" for specialist)

### AC-2: AI generates contextual follow-up questions

- **Given**: A patient provides a response to an intake question
- **When**: The response is processed by Gemini AI (gemini-1.5-flash)
- **Then**: Within 3 seconds, a relevant follow-up question is generated based on the response content, medical context, and remaining required intake fields, with max 10 questions per session

### AC-3: Structured data extraction from conversational responses

- **Given**: The patient completes the conversational intake
- **When**: The session ends
- **Then**: AI extracts structured data (symptoms, duration, severity 1-10, medications, allergies) from the free-text conversation and maps it to standard intake form fields with confidence scores

### AC-4: Intake completion persisted and available to provider

- **Given**: A patient completes the AI intake
- **When**: The provider views the patient's pre-appointment summary
- **Then**: Both the structured extracted data and the raw conversation transcript are available, with AI-extracted fields highlighted and editable by the provider

### AC-5: Gemini API rate limiting and fallback

- **Given**: The system is under load approaching Gemini's 15 RPM free-tier limit
- **When**: A new intake session is started
- **Then**: Requests are queued and processed within rate limits; if the queue exceeds 30 seconds wait time, the patient is offered the manual intake form (us_017) as an alternative

## Edge Cases

- Patient provides medically alarming response (e.g., "chest pain right now") → AI responds with "Please call 911 or go to your nearest ER immediately" and flags the intake for urgent staff review
- Gemini API returns malformed/empty response → retry once; if still fails, present the next predetermined question from the intake template
- Patient abandons intake mid-conversation → save progress; resume from last question on next visit within 48 hours; expire incomplete intakes after appointment passes
